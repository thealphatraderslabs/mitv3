STATS
SuperTrend
BULLISH = price is in an uptrend right now. BEARISH = price is in a downtrend. This is the most straightforward trend signal.
EFF. Ratio
Efficiency Ratio — measures how "clean" the price move is. High (0.7+) = price is moving in a clear direction. Low (under 0.3) = price is choppy and directionless. CHOPPY label means wait before trading.
Trend QI
Trend Quality Index — overall quality of the current trend. HIGH (0.6+) = strong, clean trend worth following. LOW = weak or noisy trend, higher risk.
RSI (14)
Relative Strength Index — measures if a coin is overbought or oversold. Below 30 = oversold (potential bounce). Above 70 = overbought (potential pullback). 40–60 = neutral zone.
Overall Bias
The terminal's combined verdict. BULLISH = more reasons to buy than sell. BEARISH = more reasons to sell. Confidence % shows how strong that view is.
Derivatives BINANCE FUTURES
Funding Rate
A small fee paid between traders every 8 hours. Positive = longs paying shorts (market may be over-bought). Negative = shorts paying longs (market may be oversold).
Open Interest
Total number of active bets (contracts) open right now. Rising OI + rising price = strong trend. Falling OI = traders closing positions.
Long / Short Ratio
How many traders are betting the price goes up vs down. A very high ratio (everyone is long) can actually be a warning — when everyone is on one side, a reversal is more likely.
Liquidation Zones
Price levels where traders with borrowed money would be forced to close their positions. Price often moves toward these zones like a magnet.
Price Spread
Difference between the futures price and spot price. A big spread means traders are paying a premium for leveraged positions.

Market Structure & Price Action —
Trend (ST)
SuperTrend — a direction indicator. Green BULLISH means price is above the trend line and rising. Red BEARISH means price is below and falling. Think of it as the market's current mood.
BOS / CHoCH
Break of Structure / Change of Character. A BOS means the market continued its trend (a high/low was broken). A CHoCH means the trend may be flipping — a warning that direction could reverse.
Swing High / Low
The most recent significant peak (swing high) and trough (swing low) in price. These act as natural targets and stop-loss reference points.
Premium Zone
The top 25% of the current price range. Price here is expensive — better to look for shorts or wait for a pullback before buying.
Discount Zone
The bottom 25% of the current price range. Price here is cheap — better zone to look for longs / buying opportunities.
Fair Value Gaps
Price gaps where the market moved so fast that no real trading happened. Price tends to revisit these zones to "fill" them — useful as entry targets.
Multi-TF Scan
The same coin analysed across different timeframes (5m, 1h, 4h etc.). When multiple timeframes agree on a direction, the signal is much stronger.

Trade Scenarios UNBIASED · BOTH DIRECTIONS
Primary Trade
The main suggested trade based on the highest-confidence direction. This is what the majority of signals are pointing to.
Counter Trade
The opposite direction trade, shown for completeness. It has low confidence — only consider it if you see additional confirmation on a smaller timeframe.
Entry Zone
The ideal price range to enter the trade. If price hasn't reached here yet, wait — entering too early reduces your edge.
Stop Loss (SL)
The price where the trade idea is proven wrong. Always set your stop here — it protects you from large losses.
TP 1 / TP 2
Take Profit levels. TP1 is the first realistic target (consider taking partial profits here). TP2 is the extended target if momentum continues.
Risk · Reward (R)
How much you could gain vs how much you risk. 3R means you could make 3× what you're risking. Higher is better — look for 2R minimum.
Conviction
HIGH = strong signal, multiple factors agree. MED = decent signal, some caution needed. LOW = weak signal, avoid or use very small size.
Setup Rejected
The engine found no clean trade opportunity right now. This is not a bad thing — it means the conditions aren't right. Wait for a better setup.

Market Classification Badges
BRK
BREAKOUT
Price is pushing through a key resistance or support level it previously could not pass. Signals the start of a strong directional move. Act fast — breakouts can move quickly.
SQZ
SQUEEZE
Price has been coiling sideways in a tight range — like a compressed spring. Volatility is low now but a big move is building. Direction is not yet confirmed — wait for a clear breakout before entering.
TRD
TREND CONTINUATION
This coin is already in a clean, established trend (up or down). The signal is to ride the existing momentum. Highest reliability — trade in the direction of the trend only.
Market State Badges (on Ranked cards)
TRD
TREND
Price is moving cleanly in one direction with consistent higher-highs (up) or lower-lows (down). Supertrend and structure agree. Strong conditions for momentum trades.
RNG
RANGE
Price is bouncing between a ceiling (resistance) and a floor (support) without breaking either. No clear direction — wait for a breakout, or trade the range boundaries if you're experienced.
TRN
TRANSITION
The market just broke structure (BOS or CHoCH) and may be switching direction. A trend could be starting — but it's not confirmed yet. Higher risk — wait for follow-through before committing.
EXP
EXPANSION
Price broke out of a range and is now moving fast and far in one direction — a full expansion move is underway. Strong momentum, but entering late in expansion is risky — the move may be nearly done.
